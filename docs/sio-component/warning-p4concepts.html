P4 guitar concepts

Caution

This resource is intended for guitar player informed, curious wishing to go beyond the framework of traditional open tuning : E-A-D-G_B-E for regular tuning, particularly all fourths tuning : E-A-D-G-C-F (or equivalent as Eb-Ab-Db-Gb-B-E)

Table 1. illustration 4ths tuning vs Tradi tuning

All 4ths tuning

E-A-D-G-C-F

Traditional open tuning

E-A-D-G_B-E

Regularity shape ←→ musical structure

Irregularity shape ←→ musical structure

One shape denote one musical structure

One shape for three musical structures : relation shape with musical structure depends of shape position on neck. This is the reality of traditional tuning.

Be careful, switching to all fourths tuning is a break with tradition and is not suitable for all uses !

If you plan to exactly reproduce guitar shots or accompany yourself on the guitar, we advise you to skip your way from this website.
